Designing the guidelines for the Blind and Keyboard Users

Intuit gave me the opportunity to work with their accessibility team as an Accessibility Product Designer Intern. I focused on designing and expanding the blind design guidelines for them to apply onto their products for their customers.

Designing a Trivia game for a Shoe Museum

The Bata Shoe Museum has many different kinds of shoes but it can overwhelming especially with so much there to digest. However, individuals can apply their knowledge from the museum on a trivia game.

Designing an Ice Cream Vending Machine for Everyone; Including your Pets

These vending machines will deliver soft serve ice-cream to support the demand in America for those who are immensely in love with their pets. They will be able to stop by and pay the machine and retrieve their ice cream for themselves and their pet to enjoy together.

Designing an Inclusive Investing Exerpience

Fidelity Investments gave me the opportunity to work with their team, BELONG, as an Inclusive User Experience Design Researcher. I focused on developing an inclusive concept for their mobile Cash Management.
